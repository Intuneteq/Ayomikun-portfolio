Blaq

Your ultimate online
sneaker destination

Blaq is an online home to all sneakers, and the sole purpose of this app is to be the ultimate source for sneakers drop ranging from nike, adidas, puma, converse, jordan and many more brands, and getting them at great deals. This platform provides a service that allows mobile users to get their fav1.5orite sneakers all in one place.

The challenge

The key challange to solve was to provide an online
platform that serves as a getway to mobile users to get
their favorites sneakers in one place at great deals and
discount without having to move through different app or
leave the comfort of their home in order to search for this
sneakers.

Easy Sign up

To achieve a straightforward sign up experience, it was
important that I came up with a page that is easy but also
collects the required data of the user.

Seamless
process

To achieve a seamless payment processsing, I came up
with a flow that is easy to understand by the user. I
streamlined the entire flow into successive steps as the
user progress through the application. I also added short
sentence that will enable the user to understand what
they are doing at each particular stage.

Detailed
Confirmation page

In order to have a order completion page that is detailed
and also not congested but easy to understand. I made
use of a confirmation page that captures what the user
ordered, the number of days to delivery, a track order link
and also a button that takes the user to the home page to
continue shopping. This page ensures the user gets the
important information about their order.
